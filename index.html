Covid-19 Tweets' Topic Modelling

Visualizations, containing analysis of Covid-19 related tweets, will be displayed.

The purpose of this project is to visualize the topics from the tweets that are related to coronavirus. There are positive and negative tweets, related to the pandemic, and the web application would show the visualization of the topics, from those positive and negative tweets. Sentiment analysis will be performed on the tweets, in order to determine whether the tweets are positive, negative, or neutral. The application would allow people to see the visualization of topics in positive, negative, and neutral tweets, and through that viewers can understand the positive and negative impact of coronavirus on the people environment and ways people are tweeting about coronavirus.
